Vision

Vision of the Real Charitable Trust is to protect the human with all rights and human dignity through basic education and humanistic service to the orphan, children, youth, women and aged persons in difficult circumstances and in distress in rural and urban. Secondly focused on protecting the environment, our Mother earth through solid waste management programmes, guarding nature with the planting and controlling pollution by awareness programmes.
